SUPERMAN

Superman is depicted as the "living God" on Earth as he is from another planet Krypton. His powers include flight, super strength, x-ray vision, invulnerability, speed, heat vision, freezing breath, super flare (a recently added massive, omnidirectional heat blast that obliterates anything within a quarter mile radius), and superhuman senses. Superman can travel at the speed of light on par with the Flash. The limits of these abilities vary depending on the writer. However, as his name suggests, Superman has frequently been depicted achieving feats of immeasurable magnitude.

He is vulnerable to Green Kryptonite, red solar radiation,lead,psionics and magic. Superman's powers come from his alien biology, but he is as vulnerable to magic as any mortal, and can easily fall under the influence of spells, potions, and curses. He lives in Metropolis.
