BLACK WIDOW

The Black Widow, aka Natalia Romanova, is one of the Marvel Universe’s fiercest fighters and stealthiest spies. Making her comic book debut way back in 1964 in an issue of Tales of Suspense, the Black Widow was originally a femme fatale who made trouble for Iron Man and his Avengers. Over time, she became an ally, an Avenger, and even a S.H.I.E.L.D. agent. As a result, Natasha held her own against gods, aliens, and otherwise enhanced human beings in battles that spanned decades. It makes sense that she’s a master martial artist and an impressive marksman. She’s trained in a variety of fighting styles and in how to use countless weapons. She has a lot of other powers and abilities and that readers and movie fans alike might not remember though.
